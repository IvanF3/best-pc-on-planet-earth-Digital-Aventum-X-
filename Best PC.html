Digital Storm Aventum X Custom Hardware Tiering

A deep dive into pricing variations, structural chassis levels, and the monster AMD Ryzen Threadripper platform.

1. The Aventum Custom Case & Chassis Options

The core structural body of the Aventum X spans 28.3 inches tall, 25.7 inches long, and weighs up to 75 pounds due to its industrial-grade construction steel and dual tempered glass panels.

  • Standard Matte Black or White Finish: Base level structural engineering steel paneling. [Included in Base Price]
  • Premium Colored Finishes: Options include specialized Fire Engine Red or Deep Sapphire Blue paint coats. [+$299 Option]
  • Custom Hydro-Dip Finish: Advanced Multi-Layer Midnight Camo patterns with highly protective High-Definition UV Gloss coats applied over the panels. [+$799 Option]

2. Advanced AMD Ryzen Threadripper Processors

The system utilizes the server-grade TRX50 and WRX90 motherboard platforms to feed massive quantities of electricity into heavy multi-core workstation and creator chips.

Tier 1: High-End Desktop Entry

The AMD Ryzen Threadripper 9965WX processor chip. It features 24 cores, 48 computational threads, and 128 Megabytes of L3 cache memory architecture specifically tuned to accelerate heavy data streaming and local AI processing speeds.

Estimated Processor Tier Cost: +$2,899 premium upgrade

Tier 2: Workstation Powerhouse

The AMD Ryzen Threadripper 9975WX creator chip. This processor utilizes 32 cores, 64 hyper-threaded execution threads, and speeds up 3D modeling, heavy cinematic rendering, and complex engineering simulations.

Estimated Processor Tier Cost: +$4,099 premium upgrade

Tier 3: The Multi-Threaded Monster

The AMD Ryzen Threadripper 9995WX ultimate workstation chip. This massive processor chip utilizes a world-record 96 execution cores, 192 dedicated computational threads, 384 Megabytes of cache memory, and pulls a massive 350 Watts of continuous electric power.

Estimated Processor Tier Cost: +$11,699 absolute extreme calculation upgrade

3. Graphics Accelerator Configurations

Graphics setups range from single high-end accelerators up to dual-linked structural parallel computational platforms.

  • Single GPU Setup: Powered by 1x NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5090 graphics card with 32 Gigabytes of high-speed video memory. [+$2,000 Component addition]
  • Workstation Dual GPU Setup: Powered by 2x NVIDIA GeForce RTX GPUs linked together physically to enable high-resolution complex artificial intelligence computations and absolute maximum cinematic performance. [+$4,500 Component addition]

4. Power and Exotic Cooling System Options

The price changes significantly depending on the structural fluid lines required to handle the heat output generated by extreme hardware combinations.

  • Standard Liquid Loop: HydroLux PRO premium hardline transparent tubes cooling only the main central processor chip. [Included in Base System Configuration]
  • Dual Loop System: Advanced fluid distribution blocks feeding separate custom fluid flows directly into both the processing chip and multiple graphics boards. Includes support for dual 560-millimeter liquid radiators. [+$600 Fluid Routing Upgrade]
  • Extreme Power Delivery: High-Capacity 2500W Silverstone Hela modular power brick designed specifically to supply clean energy to multiple graphics accelerators simultaneously. [+$350 Power Upgrade]

Pricing for fully configured custom Threadripper systems ranges from an entry pricing of roughly $10,888 up to absolute performance workstation heights of over $20,000 depending on choice of chips and finish options.
